Goulash

Description

This meaty goulash recipe is easy to make on the stovetop but can also be done in a slow cooker.

Ingredients

  • Onion and oil
  • Beef
  • Tomato products
  • Sauces and seasonings
  • Macaroni

Steps

  1. Cook the onion in oil, and then add the ground beef and cook until brown. Add garlic
  2. Stir in the water, tomatoes, and seasonings. Bring to a simmer
  3. Stir in uncooked noodles, cover, and simmer until pasta is tender
